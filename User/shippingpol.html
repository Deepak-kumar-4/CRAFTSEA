Shipping Policy!


This Shipping Policy governs the terms and conditions related to the shipping and delivery of orders placed on the Craftsea website. By placing an order, you agree to comply with the following shipping terms:

Order Processing Time:

  • Orders will be processed and prepared for shipment within 4-6 business days after payment has been successfully received and verified.
  • Business days exclude weekends and public holidays.
Shipping Destinations:
  • Craftsea ships orders to various destinations
  • If your location is not included in the available shipping destinations, please contact our customer support team for further assistance.
Shipping Costs:
  • Shipping costs are calculated based on factors such as the destination, weight, dimensions, and value of the order.
  • The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process on the Craftsea website.
Undeliverable or Returned Packages:
  • If a package is deemed undeliverable due to an incorrect or incomplete address provided by the customer, the customer will be responsible for any additional shipping costs for re-shipment.
  • If a package is returned to Craftsea due to non-delivery or failure to claim the package, the customer may be responsible for return shipping costs and may be subject to a restocking fee.
